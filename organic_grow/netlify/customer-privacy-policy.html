Privacy Policy

RiFresh Customer App

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the RiFresh Customer App. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our grocery and essentials delivery platform.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, and email address for account creation.
  • Location Data: We collect your delivery addresses to fulfill your orders accurately.
  • Order History: Information about the products you purchase.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Processing and delivering your grocery orders.
  • Providing order updates and customer support.
  • Improving app functionality and personalizing your experience.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We only share necessary details (like your delivery address and phone number) with our registered local vendors and delivery partners strictly for fulfilling your order.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and personal data. To do so, please visit our Account Deletion Page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact us at support@rifresh.in.
