Privacy Policy

RiFresh Delivery App (Driver App)

Last updated: June 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information specifically for delivery partners using the RiFresh Delivery App.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: Name, phone number, and bank account details for payouts.
  • KYC Documents: Identity proofs such as Aadhar Card and Driving License required for verification.
  • Location Data: The app collects background and foreground location data to assign nearby orders, track active deliveries, and calculate payouts.

3. How We Use Location Data

Location data is a core requirement for the RiFresh Delivery App. We use it to:

  • Notify you of incoming orders near your current location.
  • Provide live tracking to the customer while you are on an active delivery.

4. Data Protection

Your KYC documents and payout details are securely stored and are only accessible by authorized administrators. We do not share your private documents with any third party.

5. Account Deletion

You can request to delete your delivery partner account and remove your personal and KYC data from our systems by visiting our Account Deletion Page.
